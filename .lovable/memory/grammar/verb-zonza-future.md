---
name: Verb Zonza Future Tense
description: Future tense conjugation of zonza (to speak) — affirmative with mbo + subject prefix, negative with ka...ko frame
type: feature
---
Future tense of ZONZA (to speak / to talk with someone):

Affirmative (marqueur `mbo` + préfixe sujet):
- Mbo ni zonza = I will speak
- Mbo zonza = You will speak
- Mbo ka zonza = He/she will speak
- Mbo tu zonza = We will speak
- Mbo lu zonza = You (pl) will speak
- Mbo ba zonza = They will speak

Negative (cadre `ka...ko`, sans `mbo`):
- Ka ni zonza ko = I will not speak
- Ku zonza ko = You will not speak
- Ka zonza ko = He/she will not speak
- Ka tu zonza ko = We will not speak
- Ka lu zonza ko = You (pl) will not speak
- Ka ba zonza ko = They will not speak

Note: `zonzela` = parler ensemble, parler avec quelqu'un, parler de.
